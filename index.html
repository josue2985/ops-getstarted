Step 3: Configure your Discount Settings

Based on your subscription level your account will populate the appropriate discount areas for you to complete within TraxPartners. Here’s how it works!

TraxPartners is the central hub where you can manage all your account settings in one location. Here you can invite a new wholesale customer or remove an existing customer. You even have the ability to customize your discount settings by individual wholesale customer.

Here is a simple walk through on how to get to the discount settings within your TraxPartners console.

  • Go to your TraxPartner interface
  • Click on Account Settings
  • Enter the appropriate discount setting based on your subscription level, and click on “Force Update” option to update all wholesale customers.
  • You can also set up your discount by individual account by selecting the customer in TraxPartners and entering the desired discount to be applied to each customer.

Please note: If you are on a Manufacturer Conquest Program you discount setting will only apply to eligible parts.

Tap to View

Important! Not all manufacturer programs allow editable discount settings.

Important! DMS Set up is required by some MSOs and for many OPS programs, including ValuTrax Claims, and Mechanical Platforms.

Step 4: Send an Invite to your wholesale customers

Invite your wholesale customer to be connected to your account and begin sending parts orders immediately. It’s the fastest and easiest way to drive more parts sales.

Here is a simple walk through on how to send an invitation to your wholesale customer within TraxPartners.

  • Log into OPSTrax and click the TraxPartners tab at the top of the screen
  • Click on Invite New Partner
  • Follow the easy step by step instructions to complete the appropriate section and click submit.
Tap to View

Important! If you do not have a DMS integration then you will need to manually create your accounts within the TraxPartners invite form to send invitations.
