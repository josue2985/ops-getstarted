Step 3: Configure your Discount Settings

Based on your subscription level your account will populate the appropriate discount areas for you to complete within TraxPartners. Here’s how it works!

The TraxPartners console screen is your central command for managing your customer relationships. Here you can invite new customers to interact with your account, you can add configure discount settings for each customer, and “unpartner” customers if you no longer do business with them.

Here is a simple walkthrough on how to get to the ValuTrax discount settings within your TraxPartners console.

  • Go to your TraxPartner interface
  • Click on “Account Settings”
  • Adjust your ValuTrax program discount settings, use “Force Update” option to update all shop customers to your configured pricing.

Please note: The ValuTrax discounts will only apply to ValuTrax eligible parts orders. They will not impact your regular discounts.

Tap to View

Important! Not all manufacturer programs allow editable discount settings.

Important! DMS Setup is required to begin processing ValuTrax claims.
